QFS to Operate Privately:

QFS (Quantum Financial System)

The National Government body has granted official approval for a QFS company to operate as a private entity within its jurisdiction. This authorization marks a significant step in the country’s evolving approach to digital finance and blockchain innovation.


Under the go-ahead order, the company is permitted to conduct cryptocurrency-related operations — such as trading, blockchain development, digital asset management, or payment services — in compliance with the country’s financial and data protection laws. The move signifies a recognition of the company’s legal standing and operational readiness, while ensuring regulatory oversight to prevent illicit activities and maintain consumer protection.

This decision demonstrates the government’s willingness to embrace fintech innovation while balancing privacy, regulation, and economic opportunity in the emerging crypto sector.
